    Statue of Liberty

    The Statue of Liberty or Statue of Freedom is a giant statue on Liberty Island, the mouth of the Hudson River, New York Harbor, United States. This statue was a gift from France to the United States in the late 19th century and is a welcome symbol for visitors, immigrants and returning Americans.
